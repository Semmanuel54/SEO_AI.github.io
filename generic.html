Secondly, as the regional data analyst in TGI Groups, I led a performance transition scheme through critical analysis and problem-solving of a reoccurring low-performance trend. The situation involved the validation of data pre-processing methods, and communication of business insight to all stakeholders involved both from technical and non-technical backgrounds in the most efficient approach to ensure business insight implementation which yielded over 50% increment in performance. This was successfully done using SQL for database query, Power Query for ETL and Microsoft Power BI for proper visualization and report dashboards.
